Seismic Testing of RC Full Scale Structures - Ongoing

Research Assistants: Margaritis Tonidis, Gaurav Chobe, Dheeraj Waghmare, Deepak Suthar, Luis David Fernandez Vasquez, Prathyusha Malladi, Fasih Ur Rehman, Emmanuel Oyakojo

Seismic tests on two full-scale, two-story reinforced concrete moment-resisting frame structures will take place during Fall 2024 and Spring 2025 at the Bowen Laboratory in West Lafayette, Indiana, USA. The two structures are identical except the retrofitting and they represent existing non-seismically detailed structures, with no transverse reinforcement provided in the joint cores. The first structure will be in as-built condition to investigate its seismic behavior with vulnerable joints. The second structure, with identical design, will be retrofitted with the Fully Fastened Haunch Retrofit Solution, where steel haunches are post-installed onto the frame members using adhesive anchors. For the first time, this retrofit solution will be examined on 3D full-scale structural level under reversed loading conditions. The concept and test method are showcased and the implications of such test towards the improvement of seismic resilience are elucidated.
