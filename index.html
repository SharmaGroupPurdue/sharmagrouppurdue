Our Mission

Our mission is to advance research in the field of reinforced concrete structures, with a focus on anchorages, bond behavior, and retrofitting techniques. We are committed to developing innovative solutions that enhance the performance and resilience of structures, particularly in the face of challenges such as earthquakes, fire, and impact.Through in-depth studies on bond and anchorage behavior under shear, tension, and other critical forces, our goal is to contribute valuable knowledge that supports safer and more durable construction practices.

Research Areas

Reinforced Concrete | Seismic | Fire | Strengthening | Anchorages | Spring Modelling | Impact | Corrosion | Advanced Materials | Bond | Computational Modelling
